welcome to the branding guidelines for sovrn holdings.

This is where you will find the most current version of the branding guidelines for sovrn and its product family. This is intended for internal use, and includes logos, fonts and colors. Additionally, there are occasional methods for applying the brand standards. If you have any questions, please ask Bret Scofield (@brettiesco).

who we are

sovrn is an advocate for and partner to around 20,000 publishers across the independent web, representing more than a million websites, who use our tools, services and analytics to grow their audience, engage their readers, and monetize their site. We own and operate one of the largest global ad exchanges that connects publishers with a complete range of third-party monetization solutions, including display, video and mobile, as well as private marketplaces and data-driven yield optimization.

our logo & usage

The sovrn logotype and bars were developed to provide the foundation for a consistent application of the sovrn brand across all products and media including interface, web, collateral, promotions and advertising. Consistently expressing the sovrn visual identity builds a strong brand awareness, allowing sovrn to be the recognized leader in its space. Please follow these guidelines for the most effective brand expression. The sovrn corporate logo is made up of both the bars and the sovrn logotype. These two elements should appear as shown here in all corporate materials, including the sovrn website and collateral materials.
